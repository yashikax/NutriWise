What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure of body fat based on your weight and height.

BMI Ranges:

  • Underweight: Less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: 18.5 to 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 to 29.9
  • Obesity - Class 1: 30 to 34.9
  • Obesity - Class 2: 35 to 39.9
  • Obesity - Class 3: 40 or greater
